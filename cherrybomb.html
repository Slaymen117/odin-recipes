CHERRY BOMB Drink

This Cherry Bomb is a wild cocktail, but before we break down
the recipe for you, here are some other energy drink cocktail recipes that
you'll probably enjoy: the Jelly Donut, the Irish Trash Can, the Energy Long
Island Iced Tea, the Jägerbomb, the Atomic Jägerbomb, and the Absinthe Bull.
As far as this Cherry Bomb cocktail recipe is concerned, this bad boy mixes up
cherry vodka, a splash of grenadine, and, of course, some energy drink — i.e. Red Bull.
It's hard to say where this Cherry Bomb mixed drink was first made or who
first made it, but we're going to go ahead and guess that it's based on
the Cherry Bomb firework. It is a bomb shot after all.... If you're looking
for other bomb shot cocktail recipes, check out these other Red Bull
mixed drink recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz. (30ml) CHERRY VODKA
  • 1/2 PINT ENERGY DRINK
  • splash GRENADINE

Steps to Make It

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Pour the vodka into an ​ice-filled highball glass.
  3. Top with equal amounts of energy drink and grenadine
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge. Serve and enjoy.
